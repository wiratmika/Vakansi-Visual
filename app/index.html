Hello,

We are here to tell your story

Hello! We are a creative label based in Jakarta who loves to tell story, your story!

VAKANSI VISUAL is a Jakarta based creative label committed to accommodate clients’ need to tell their stories through creative, personalized, and high quality video. A brainchild of Adhyatmika and Annisa Amalia, VAKANSI VISUAL is a collaborative production within creative professionals from different fields of media background.

We work as an integrated studio. Our mission is to provide our clients with one stop service. Starting from the creative conception, to the finishing touch, and execute it with good story and tasteful design.

Personalization is our key. We aim to engage consumer’s personal experience.

VAKANSI VISUAL started with the foundation that art and business must walk hand in hand. We have our Original Content where we produced short films, music videos, and feature films. We also created a webseries, The Influencers, that highlights wonderful people who work in creative industry in Indonesia.

In our Commercial & Services division, we offer services such as (and not limited to) company profile, TV commercial, brand video, and sponsored webseries that blend idealistic nature of filmmaking and strategic thinking of business.

With the spirit of creativity and professionalism, we are here to understand your perspective, your excitement for your product, your passionate movement for a cause, or your celebration for your business.

We will ensure that each frame reflects your authentic vision, your essence, your story.

We are here to tell personal stories that engage the audience with the product, which makes it relatable and memorable.

Sometime around 2013 they decided to team up and founded VAKANSI VISUAL. From there, the story continues...
